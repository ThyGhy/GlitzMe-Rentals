Our Story

Creating Magical Moments in Las Vegas Since 2021

GlitzMe Rentals was born from a passion for creating unforgettable experiences and bringing joy to every event. As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of those special moments that bring people together.

What started as a small collection of party rentals has grown into Las Vegas's premier source for event equipment and experiences. Our commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for schools, churches, and families throughout the Las Vegas Valley.
